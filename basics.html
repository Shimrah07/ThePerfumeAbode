Perfume 101:
A Guide to Choosing Your Perfect Scent

Fragrance Families

Perfumes are categorized into families such as Floral, Woody, Oriental, Fresh, Gourmand and Fruity
Each family evokes a unique mood and style.

Notes in Perfumes

Perfumes are composed of three layers:

  • Top Notes: The first impression, lasting 10-15 minutes.
  • Middle Notes: The heart of the fragrance, lasting several hours.
  • Base Notes: The long-lasting foundation of the scent.

Perfume Types

  • Eau de Parfum (EDP): Strong and long-lasting, ideal for special occasions.
  • Eau de Toilette (EDT): Lighter and perfect for daily use.
  • Eau de Cologne: Refreshing and subtle for a quick pick-me-up.

How to Apply

Apply perfume to pulse points like the wrists, neck, or behind the ears to enhance its longevity and projection.
